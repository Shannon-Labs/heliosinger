### Full description

Heliosinger transforms live NOAA space weather data into a premium ambient audio experience.

Monitor solar conditions in real time, hear intensity shifts through continuous playback, and stay informed with configurable alerts designed for practical daily use.

What you get:

- Live solar weather snapshots from NOAA sources
- Ambient audio mapped to geomagnetic and flare conditions
- Flare timeline with concise impact summaries
- Learning cards that explain what current conditions mean
- Quiet hours, alert thresholds, and background playback controls

Heliosinger is built for people who want a calm, useful signal from space weather without noise.
